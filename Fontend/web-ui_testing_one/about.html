About

Depression is a mental health issue which affects millions of people around the world. It is characterized by feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and worthlessness. Depression can be triggered by various factors such as genetics, trauma, and substance abuse.

The symptoms of depression can vary from person to person. Some people may experience some or all of the following:

  • Loss of interest in activities they used to enjoy
  • Weight loss or gain
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Insomnia or sleeping too much
  • Feeling irritable or experiencing mood swings
  • Inability to experience pleasure in activities that used to bring them joy
