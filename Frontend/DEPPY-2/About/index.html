About

Depression is a mental health issue which affects millions of people around the world. It is characterized by feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and worthlessness. Depression can be triggered by various factors such as genetics, trauma, and substance abuse. The symptoms of depression can vary from person to person. Some people may experience some or all of the following:

  • Loss of interest in activities they used to enjioy
  • Weight loss or gain
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Insomnia or sleeping too much
  • Freeling irritable or experncing mood swings
  • IUnability to experience pleasure in activities that used to bring them joy

Detecting depression

Our application is designed to detect depression in users using 2 simple steps.

  • Step one is answering a questionnaire
  • Step two is reacting to a few video clips

Once both steps are completed, a report will be given to the user stating whether or not the user has depression
